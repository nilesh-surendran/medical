Head

In this tab you will find illnesses, diseases, and injuries related to the head

Dry mouth
  • Dry mouth, also known as Sjogren's syndrome is an inflammatory disease in which the eyes and mouth become really dry.
  • Symptoms - Dry eyes, dry mouth, swollen salivary glands, dry cough, fatigue
  • Cures - There is no cure but there are ways to decrease the effects such as sipping water and sucking or chewing on sugarless candy and gum.
Headache
  • A headache is a feeling of pain and discomfort in your head area.
  • Symptoms - Head usually hurts on both sides, Pain is mild to moderate, but not severe
  • Cures - Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Acetaminophen, Hot or cold compresses to your head or neck, Rest in a quiet, dark room
Sensitive teeth
  • Sensitive teeth is if you eat or drink something it causes a sharp pain in your teeth.
  • Symptoms - Sharp pain after eating or drinking something
  • Cures - Desensitizing toothpaste, fluoride, root canal, or surgical gum graft
