Arms

In this tab you will find illnesses, diseases, and injuries related to the arms

Broken Arm
  • A broken arm is self explanatory, it is when a bone in the arm is broken.
  • Symptoms - Severe pain, swelling, bruising, deformity, not being able to turn your arm
  • Sling, ice, rest, realignment if needed and maybe surgery if complicated
tendonitis
  • Tendonitis is when a tendon is inflamed and it can happen anywhere there is a tendon in the body.
  • Symptoms - Pain when the tendon is moved, Swelling from fluid and inflammation, A feeling of grating when the joint is being moved
  • Cures - Surgery, antibiotics if it is because of an infection, icing the area, putting a splint and reducing movement.
