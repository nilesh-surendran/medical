Asthma
  • Asthma is when your airways will narrow up and swell. Extra mucus may also be caused. Breathing can be made difficult by this and you may wheeze.
  • Symptoms - Shortness of breath, chest tightness or pain, wheezing, coughing
  • Cures - Asthma has no cure but the symptoms can be controlled and these methods to control will be said by the doctor when you check with them
Cardiomyopathy
  • Disorders that affect the heart are called cardiomyopathies. It causes the heart to lose the ability to pump blood well
  • Symptoms - Breathlessness, swelling in the legs, ankle and feet, bloating of the abdomen, heartbeats feel rapid, chest discomfort, dizziness
  • Cures - medications, surgically implanted devices, heart surgery, and if severe a heart transplant
High Blood Pressure
  • High BP is a common condition that will affect the arteries. Another name for it is hypertension. High BP is when the blood that pushes against the artery walls is consistently really high.
  • Symptoms - Headaches, shortness of breath, nosebleeds
  • Cures - Healthy lifestyle habits that include not smoking, exercising and eating healthy. Medicine may also be needed for some people
